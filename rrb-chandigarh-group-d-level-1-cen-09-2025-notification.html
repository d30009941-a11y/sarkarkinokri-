Selection Process

The selection process involves the following stages:

  1. Computer Based Test (CBT)
  2. Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
  3. Document Verification
  4. Medical Examination

Physical Efficiency Test (PET) Standards

Candidates must meet the following PET standards:

  • Male Candidates: Lift & Carry 35kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes and Run 1000 meters in 4 min 15 sec
  • Female Candidates: Lift & Carry 20kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes and Run 1000 meters in 5 min 40 sec
